About Us

From our strategic base in Kolkata, Krishna Chemicals has emerged as a benchmark for quality and innovation in the chemical manufacturing sector. We are more than just suppliers; we are partners in our clients' success, providing bespoke solutions and cutting-edge products like our 'Roja' and 'White Horse' brands.


Our philosophy is built on a foundation of rigorous R&D, ethical practices, and a deep commitment to environmental stewardship. We believe in creating chemistry that not only performs but also preserves.
